**CRITICAL: You MUST call the MCP tool `mcp__mework-mcp__list_child_sandboxes` with
`parent_id: "mework-dev"`.** Do NOT answer from memory.

The sandbox list is maintained by the MCP server, not by your session tracking.
Always call the tool with your parent_id filter and report its results.

Present the results as a bullet list. For each sandbox, report its name,
status (running/done), and result. Highlight any that have finished.
